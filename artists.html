Our Artists

Discover the talented creators who make up the NA Collective roster. Each member brings a unique perspective, blending influences from hip‑hop, R&B, experimental electronica and beyond.

Noah Pullen – Nascent

Nascent is the stage name of Iowa City–based producer and vocalist Noah Pullen. With a penchant for introspective lyricism and experimental beats, Nascent blurs the line between electronic sound design and heartfelt storytelling. Raised in North Liberty, he began releasing music under the moniker in his teens and quickly gained a local following for his forward‑thinking productions and soulful hooks. Outside of the studio he’s an aspiring multimedia artist, creating visuals to accompany his releases.

Ahmed Awad – Hamudi

Better known by his stage name Hamudi, Ahmed Awad brings a melodic rap style rooted in his Palestinian heritage and Midwestern upbringing. His smooth delivery and knack for catchy hooks complement Nascent’s production, creating a synergy that’s both hypnotic and uplifting. Hamudi’s lyrics weave between bilingual rhymes, soulful melodies and introspective bars. On stage he commands attention with a magnetic presence and an ear for engaging audiences of all backgrounds.

About NA Collective

NA Collective began as a passion project between Nascent and Hamudi in 2018 when the two artists met at an open mic in Iowa City. What started as informal jam sessions soon evolved into a community for musicians, visual artists and storytellers. The duo invited collaborators from across Iowa’s underground scene, including experimental producer SidTiva and soulful vocalist M.E.D., nurturing a creative incubator focused on authenticity and experimentation. Today the collective continues to push boundaries through music, art and live events while staying deeply connected to their Midwestern roots.
