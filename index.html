About

Music built
with intention.

Raised in Iowa and shaped by four years of service in the United States Marine Corps, Noah Pullen brings discipline, depth, and intention to every record he creates. What began as a personal outlet evolved into a distinct artistic voice—one rooted in truth, craftsmanship, and the belief that music should leave something behind long after the last note fades.

Blending conscious lyricism with modern trap energy, melodic atmosphere, funk textures, and boom bap foundations, Noah's sound balances introspection with impact. His music pulls from multiple worlds—raw storytelling, cinematic production, experimental sound design, and emotionally honest songwriting.

Every release is built with intention, from pocket-driven flows and layered cadences to carefully selected sonic textures that feel both modern and timeless. Drawing from real-life experiences, personal growth, faith, sacrifice, love, loss, and transformation, Noah creates music for listeners who value substance as much as sound.

More than a genre, Noah Pullen is building a body of work—one record at a time.
