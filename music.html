Music Releases

Listen to our latest tracks and dive into the sonic world of NA Collective. Each release is a collaboration between Nascent’s textural production and Hamudi’s melodic storytelling.

Take My Time (feat. Hamudi)

Nascent × Hamudi • Single

A slow‑burning collaboration where Nascent’s shimmering pads and sub‑bass lay the foundation for Hamudi’s introspective melodies. The track builds from a sparse opening into a lush chorus that invites repeated listens. Released in late 2024, it marks the duo’s first official single as NA Collective.

Dominoes (feat. Hamudi)

Nascent × Hamudi • Single

An upbeat yet reflective track where cascading synth arpeggios intersect with Hamudi’s bilingual verses. “Dominoes” explores the ripple effects of our decisions and the importance of community. Infectious hooks and subtle guitar lines make this song a crowd favourite during live sets.

Limitless Pursuit (feat. Hamudi)

Nascent × Hamudi • Single

Driven by a pulsating beat and shimmering synths, “Limitless Pursuit” captures the duo at their most triumphant. The song celebrates breaking boundaries and chasing your dreams with relentless passion. Its anthemic chorus and resonant bassline showcase the collective’s growth since their inception in 2018.
